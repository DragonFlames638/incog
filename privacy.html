Incognito Privacy Policy

Last modified on 5/15/2021

Incognito is a service operated in the United States.

  • What data is being collected?

    Incognito is glad to inform you that we do not collect any of your data.
    We believe that collecting user data is infriging our goal of creating a more private internet.

  • How is Incognito a free service without selling any data?

    Incognito is a free service because its being funded by using a service called Arc.io.
    "Arc is a peer-to-peer content exchange and delivery network (CDN). Add Arc to your website in 2 minutes, your users share bandwidth, we rent it, and you get paid the proceeds."
